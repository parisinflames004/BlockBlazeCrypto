Content Automation Platform

AUTOMATED
VIDEO PUBLISHING

BlockBlazeCrypto is a short-form video automation platform that publishes content to social media platforms on behalf of a single content creator.

How It Works
END-TO-END
AUTOMATION
01

Content Generation

Video scripts are generated automatically based on trending topics relevant to the channel's niche.

02

Video Rendering

Voiceover is generated and combined with visual assets to produce a finished short-form video locally.

03

Automated Publishing

Finished videos are published directly to the creator's social media profiles via platform APIs on a set schedule.

Data & Privacy

This application is operated by a single content creator for their own account. No third-party user data is collected, stored, or processed.

The app owner and social media account owner are the same individual. All content is posted to accounts owned and controlled by the app operator.
